# Channel Role Taxonomy

Use this taxonomy to classify media touchpoint contribution.

## Journey roles

| Role | Definition | Evidence to look for | Suggested KPIs |
|---|---|---|---|
| Demand Generator | Creates or stimulates demand before users actively search or return | High first-user share, high new-user rate, early path appearances, engaged sessions, downstream Search/Direct overlap | New users, engaged sessions, assisted conversions, first-touch conversions, branded search lift, qualified visits |
| Discovery / Awareness Driver | Introduces users at broad-reach or content-led stages | Early touchpoints, broad landing pages, content engagement, video/display/social exposure | Reach, new users, engagement rate, view-through/supporting evidence, assisted paths |
| Consideration Driver | Helps users compare, research, and return | Middle path appearances, product/category/content views, repeated sessions, form starts/add_to_cart | Engaged sessions, repeat sessions, add_to_cart, form_start, product views, assisted conversions |
| Demand Capturer | Captures existing intent from users already aware or actively searching | Brand Search, high-intent Search, Direct, product landing pages, high conversion rate | Conversion rate, impression share for search, qualified conversions, session-level revenue, brand/non-brand split |
| Conversion Closer | Frequently appears immediately before conversion | Final path node, last session, checkout/form completion, high last-click conversions | Final-touch conversions, checkout completion, lead submit rate, revenue, cost per conversion |
| Assisted Contributor | Does not dominate last click but appears before converting sessions | Early/mid path presence, overlap with closing channels, conversion paths containing the channel | Assisted conversions, path inclusion rate, overlap with Search/Direct, engaged users |
| Retention / Re-engagement Driver | Brings known users back to repeat, renew, or complete action | Returning users, repeat purchases, CRM audiences, lifecycle campaigns | Returning-user conversions, repeat purchase rate, LTV, renewal rate, email/SMS revenue |
| Low Incrementality Touchpoint | Likely captures users who already intended to convert | Very late touchpoint, coupon/affiliate/retargeting dominance, low new-user share, high overlap with high-intent users | Incrementality tests, holdout lift, audience exclusions, marginal CPA, new-to-file conversions |

## Channel-specific heuristics

### Paid Search

- Separate Brand vs Non-brand whenever possible.
- Brand Search often acts as Demand Capturer or Conversion Closer.
- Non-brand Search can act as Demand Capturer or Consideration Driver depending on query intent.
- Avoid claiming Paid Search created all demand if paths show prior Paid Social, Display, Video, Organic, Referral, or Direct touchpoints.

### Organic Search

- Can serve consideration, demand capture, or discovery depending on landing page and query intent.
- Content/blog landing pages suggest discovery or consideration.
- Product/category and brand pages suggest capture.

### Direct

- Treat as a mixed channel, not a pure source of free demand.
- Direct may represent returning users, dark social, untagged campaigns, app/email leakage, cookie loss, bookmarks, or true direct navigation.
- If Direct closes many conversions, look for earlier channels and returning-user share.

### Paid Social

- Often contributes to discovery, awareness, and consideration.
- Last-click revenue may understate its influence.
- Evaluate new users, engaged sessions, assisted path presence, retargeting split, and downstream Search/Direct overlap.

### Display and Video

- Often upper-funnel or remarketing-oriented.
- Separate prospecting from retargeting if campaign naming allows.
- Evaluate reach/new users, engagement, assisted paths, and frequency; avoid last-click-only judgment.

### Email and SMS

- Usually retention, re-engagement, or conversion closing.
- Evaluate returning users, repeat purchases, lifecycle segment, revenue per recipient, and unsubscribe/fatigue if available.

### Affiliate, coupon, referral, and partners

- Separate editorial/content partners from coupon/loyalty/toolbar affiliates.
- Coupon and loyalty affiliates may be Low Incrementality Touchpoints if they appear late and overlap heavily with Direct/Brand Search.
- Content partners may generate discovery or consideration.

### Retargeting

- Can be useful consideration support or low-incrementality closing.
- Review audience definitions, recency windows, frequency, exclusions, and new vs returning user split.
